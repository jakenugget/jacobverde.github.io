Carrot Top Cakery

Carrot Top Cakery started when Maddy’s son Duke had his first birthday. She wanted to throw him an amazing birthday party with a huge cake but couldn’t afford a custom cake from a local bakery. After Dukes birthday Maddy decided it was time to learn and practiced many many times. Once perfected-Carrot Top Cakery was born! Carrot Top Cakery is a local home bakery with pricing everyone can feel good about. Now everyone can have the opportunity to give their children memorable birthday cakes without breaking the bank.

Verde Trucking

Verde trucking is a father/son truck driving duo hauling crude day and night! Ever wonder how gas stations get your gasoline? Oil (crude) is pumped up from the ground and truck drivers, just like Jared and Scott with Verde Trucking, take that oil and haul it to a refinery. That refinery then processes that oil and turns it into so many different things, including gasoline! The job can be dangerous, but the Verde men take lots of precautions to get the job done! Every time you fill up your car with gasoline just remember YOU are supporting small businesses all over America!
