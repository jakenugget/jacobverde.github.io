Target Audience

The target audience is a small town that doesn't have its own news station.

A rancher who has a large amount of land that has been his families for 100 years.

An elementary aide who watches the children during recess.

Scenarios

  1. Winter weather that can be well below freezing due to inversion.
  2. Tornado touching down near the small town.
  3. Dense fog during the winter dropping visibility.
  4. Summer rain storms that would mess with harvesting crops.
  5. High wind warning during the county fair.
